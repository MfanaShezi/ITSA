About Us

who are we

ITSA is a student association that operates under the sponsorship of the University of the Free State (UFS). ITSA is managed by students of the Computer Science and Informatics Department of the UFS on a not-for-profit basis, and is responsible for the organisation of various events, both social and academic, for students interested in IT. Members of ITSA are students of the UFS (Membership), with staff members of the Department of Computer Science and Informatics serving as honorary members. ITSA student members must be enrolled for at least one CSIS module, not including CSIL.

our mission

The mission of ITSA is to give students wishing to be employable in the IT industry, once they have finished studying, a platform to learn new skills and learn about the industry itself. In addition to this, ITSA is there for IT students to socialise and get to know each other.

our vision

The vision of ITSA is to provide a platform for IT students to come together, fostering a culture of technological innovation at UFS, especially among students from the Computer Science and Informatics Department.
